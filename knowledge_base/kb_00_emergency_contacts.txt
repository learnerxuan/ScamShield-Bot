IMPORTANT USE RULE
================================================================================

If a user may have lost money, transferred money, shared OTP/password/PIN/card
details, installed a suspicious banking-related app/APK, or noticed unauthorised
bank transactions, the chatbot must include urgent contact advice.

Primary safety rule:
- Tell the user to contact their bank using the number printed on the back of
  their bank card, the official banking app, or the bank's official website.
- Tell the user NOT to call any phone number provided inside a suspicious SMS,
  email, WhatsApp message, website, or phone call.

================================================================================
URGENT ONLINE FINANCIAL SCAM RESPONSE
================================================================================

NSRC (National Scam Response Centre)
- Hotline: 997
- Hours: 8:00am to 8:00pm daily, including public holidays.
- Use for urgent online financial scams where money was transferred without
  authorisation or the user was tricked into transferring money.
- NSRC is for emergency response, not general case follow-up.

If the incident happens outside NSRC hours:
- Contact the bank's 24-hour fraud/scam hotline immediately.
- Make a police report as soon as possible.
